AIR POLLUTION IN ATTIKI - GREECE

A Few Words on Local Air Pollution

Due to the special topography of Athens basin, studying air Pollution is challening. More specifically, there are 4 main mountains (Parnitha, Penteli, Imittos, Aigaleo) surrounding the densely populated Athens basin. These mountains from highest to lowest are:

  1. Parnitha
  2. Penteli
  3. Imittos
  4. Aigaleo

It is essential to take into account atmospheric stability in order to interpret pollution diagrams
